Who are we?

We are the voices of tomorrow!

Inspired by award-winning films - Before the Flood and Cowspiracy, we started this page in 2020 to raise awareness about the threat of climate change.

We have seen a rising number of documentaries and activists raising their concern on climate change. In particular, the production of a harmful gas methane through livestocks!

With temperature on the rise, the question is, what can we do to play our part?


What can we do?

  1. Stop beef consumption

  2. Do your research

  3. Raise awareness
    • Tell your loved ones
    • Lead by example

  4. Join our 30 days challenge!

  5. Just start somewhere!
